Golden Month Event

Golden Month is coming to Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, and it’s going to be one of the most exciting events of the season! There will also be spin events and limited-time challenges where you can win amazing prizes. It’s the perfect time to log in every day and enjoy extra bonuses with your friends.
