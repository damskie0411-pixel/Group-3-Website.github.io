Hero Roles & Tips

Gold Lane

Focus on farming and scaling. Usually marksmen like Claude or Karrie.

EXP Lane

Frontliners and bruisers that sustain and win duels.

Mid Lane

Mages that rotate and apply map pressure.

Jungle

Map control, objective securing, and tempo.

Roam

Protect allies, create vision, and engage fights.
